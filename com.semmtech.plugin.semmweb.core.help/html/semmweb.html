SEMMweb User Guide

SEMMweb™ is the Semantic Web software created by Semmtech. SEMMweb™ allows for information to be managed and published for access via the internet. Such information could take the form of dictionaries, product catalogues, or standards that products need to conform to. Larger quantities of data, such as project data or geographical data, could also be modelled by using SEMMweb™.

A community, no matter how small or large, can link up their own model to those of others. In situations where such models will enrich your information. It’s unnecessary to maintain the same data at multiple locations. Reusing data from other models saves time and money in managing all the information. And developing additional software just to connect such models is a thing of the past.

With SEMMweb™, information can be presented to various software. Our SEMMweb™ software conforms to Open Standards, which entails that information created in it is not restricted for use in a single software package, including our own. Furthermore, the architecture of these Open Standards is so flexible that data administrators require less time and encounter fewer risks to adapt to new developments.
